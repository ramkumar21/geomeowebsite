An Innovative Solution to Integrate and centralize all your cards

A connected electronic Smart card, that helps citizens for payment, authorization, transaction, physical and virtual Id card, license, attendance card and Integrated with many other facilities that can be customized by the one who owns the card. 

Be guarded by cops, with one click ahead

A revolutionary application which tracks user’s location and sends the details to control room of police department in any case of mis-happenings. This application allows to make SOS calls, file e-complaint, update traffic status and any mis-happenings around their environment.  

A modern Hawk that keeps eye on you and your assets

An Intelligent Surveillance camera with most powerful video management platform, innovative technologies and custom solutions that converts video datas into intelligence, protecting people, infrastructure and commerce across the globe. 

Connected communities can positively impact property values and desirability.

Geo Mesh enables local communities to get connected through offline routers and servers, which can make make a community to stay online alltime and be aware of local updates. Geo Mesh technology can also be used to conduct online examinations in offline manner at rural areas.

Track anyone and anything from your mobile and PC

Geo Shadow offers an efficient, effective tracking system for managing your , vehicles, assets, company’s staff, close ones to your heart. This is a Live tracking system which improves security with GPRS, GPS and GSM system. 

A new mode of public transportation arrives

Geo Hypod is a proposed solution for advanced transportation system to reduce traffic congestion in cities, where peoples can travel with pods and shuttles in an inexpensive, efficient and hassle-less manner.
