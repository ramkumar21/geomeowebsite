About Us

WHO WE ARE & WHAT WE DO

Geo Meo Informatics, a location based intelligence company. Our vision is to renovate an intermediate city/village into a magnificent city that has excellent communication, immense security, hassle-less transportation and Instant money transaction, which can be simply titled as Smart City.

Geo Meo believes technology should inspire, amaze, energize our ambitions and help us reimagine our world. Its why we create a technology that escalates us.  

Vision

Our company’s Vision is to facilitate a city with excellent connectivity, communication, data transfer, transportation and security in a quality manner which will enhance the living quality of the people. By 2020 we will bring around five major cities into smart cities and convert villages into smart digital village.
